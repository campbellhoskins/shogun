⚡ Why a Graph, Not RAG?

RAG Approach

  • Single-hop retrieval
  • Misses cascading obligations
  • No structural reasoning
  • Hallucination risk on policy

Graph Approach

  • Multi-hop traversal
  • Complete obligation chains
  • Deterministic paths
  • Auditable decisions
